# Product Overview

**Flappy Kiro** - A Flappy Bird-style arcade game featuring the Kiro logo as the playable character.

## Core Gameplay
- Player controls a character that falls due to gravity
- Press SPACE or click to jump and navigate through obstacles
- Avoid hitting pipes or boundaries to survive
- Score increases by successfully passing through pipe gaps

## Game States
- **Start**: Welcome screen with instructions
- **Playing**: Active gameplay with physics and obstacles
- **Game Over**: End screen showing final score with restart option

## Target Experience
- Simple, responsive controls
- Balanced physics for satisfying gameplay
- Clean visual design using Kiro brand colors
- Immediate feedback and smooth 60 FPS performance
